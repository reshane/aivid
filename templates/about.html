AIVID

This site is dedicated to classifying images as poison ivy or not poison ivy. The purpose is also to empower the users to feed the model. This is done by contesting the model's classifications and voting to either uphold or overturn said classifications.

Voting

In the voting tab are a series of classifications that have been contested by users. Voting to uphold the classification will take the classification out of the 'contested' pool. Voting to overturn will ensure that when the model is retrained, the opposite outcome of this classification will be included the training data.

The AI

The model was created by training the Resnet18 pretrained convolutional neural net model from PyTorch on ~14,000 imgaes of poison ivy and other plants similar in appearance(including box elder, virginia creeper, boston ivy, and a few more). The images were collected via plantnet.org. The model was trained using the fastai python library

Purpose

The purpose is to allow users complete control over the data that the model is trained on, no matter the outcome. Happy classifying xoxo shanek
